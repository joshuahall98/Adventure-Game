Adventure Begins

You take your first steps out of the village and feel a great weight being lifted off your shoulders. Until now your entire life revolved around the happenings within the village, but now, now your time has come to create your own path. You walk down the road leaving the village, enjoying the warm sun on the back of your neck. After a short time you come to a familiar left turn in the road running alongside a nearby forest. You know if you follow this road you will reach your neighbouring village at the foot of the mountain and there you can buy some better supplies, the road is long though. Your other option is to cut through the forest which takes much less time to reach the village, you have been through there many times and the risk is low as not many dark creatures make it their home.
