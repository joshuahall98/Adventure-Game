The Village

You enter the village through the front gate, the assortment of buildings and shops greatly dwarfs the number in your own village. A dog runs by chased by a group of children and you wish you could join in but you're only here for one reason, to gather any information on the whereabouts of the sword. You know the local tavern is good for news but the shop keeper trades with alot of travelling merchants and he too could have some valuable information. The group of children run past you once more while you ponder which place to start.
