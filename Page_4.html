Cut Through the Forest

The cool climate of the forest hits you the second you enter it's shade, its quite refreshing but you were enjoying the sun. You spot the usual trail you take and commence your trek through the woods. After a short time walking you notice some movement in a nearby bush, you stop, wondering whether or not you should take out your axe, when suddenly a small furry creature jumps out! You laugh and scold yourself for being on edge as you look down at the squirrel. The small animal animal stares up at you but doesn't flee. The squirrel then weirdly beckons for you to follow it. You are curious, the squirrel is definitely giving off signals that it wants to be followed, but your plan was to reach the village as soon as possible.
