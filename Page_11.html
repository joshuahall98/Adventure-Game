The Tavern

You enter the tavern and as always it is full with people, most of the men enjoying a drink after a hard days work but you spot hooded figures here and there at the edges, thiefs, pickpockets and who knows what else. You head up to the bar, the bar man watches you as you approach through narrowed eyes, cleaning a glass with a not so clean wrag. You take a seat at the bar and you get straight to the point and ask the bar man if he has heard any news about the lost kings sword. The noise of conversation around the bar dies down slightly, obviously you are not the only one in search of the treasure. The bar man stops his cleaning, he thinks for a second.
"I have heard rumours from travelling folk, some of it true some of it not, but all of them seem to be heading to the other side of the mountain."
The bar man leans in closer.
"I've also heard tell of a man who knows a lot about the sword, but his name and whereabouts will cost ya, 10 gold or I'll be keeping my mouth shut"
