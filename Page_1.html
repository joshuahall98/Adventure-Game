Story Background

At the base of a giant mountain, surrounded by forest and fields stands a small village, it is here where are story begins. Three days ago news reached the village that the whereabouts of the crypt that held the Lost King's Sword had been found, but not the sword itself. Legend says that the wielder of the sword be given the power to rule over the land of Vanyeuor. The sword had been lost over three hundred years ago and since then no king has ruled, the lands have become a dangerous place and are overun with villainous characters who are exploiting those of lesser power than they.
Our protagonist in this story is a young man, who only recently reached manhood. His great grandfather used to tell him stories about the times when kings ruled, always reminding him that his great grandfather was the one who bestowed these stories upon him. When are young protagonist heard the news about the sword, he decided it was time to bring back the old ways. It took him the three days to pack all his essentials
